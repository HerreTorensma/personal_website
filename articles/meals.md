## Enchiladas
Ingredients:

- 400g beans in chili sauce
- 2 tortillas
- Some Gouda or Cheddar cheese
- Tomato
- Onion or avocado
- Lime or lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Greek yoghurt or sour cream

## Curry
Ingredients:

- Paneer
- Rice
- Onion
- Spinach
